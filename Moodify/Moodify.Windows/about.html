About the game

People with autism commonly have difficulties in ‘reading’ body language and facial expressions.

Moodify helps these children understand emotions and facial expressions and pictures from all over the world! This app enables users to recognize and learn basic expressions and feelings (happy, sad, scared, angry).

  • Dynamic Database
  • Soothing Music
  • Colorful

This app is a good exercise for just about anyone, thanks to a conception realized with special-need teachers and healthcare professionals, but can be used by all children to discover emotions.
